# Mobile Sales Analysis

## Project Overview

Mobile Sales Analysis is an interactive sales dashboard project designed to study mobile phone sales performance across brands, cities, customer segments, payment methods, and time periods. It helps convert raw transaction data into clear business insights so that sales trends, product performance, and customer preferences can be understood quickly and used for better decision-making.



The dashboard is useful for identifying top-selling products, monitoring revenue trends, comparing channel performance, and understanding how factors like storage, color, operating system, and customer demographics influence sales outcomes.

## Challenges Faced

One challenge in this project is organizing sales data across multiple dimensions such as brand, city, channel, payment method, and customer segment while keeping the report easy to interpret. This is typically handled by structuring the data model carefully and creating visuals that answer one business question at a time [web:3][web:13].

Another challenge is making the dashboard informative without overcrowding it. The solution is to prioritize key KPIs such as total sales, quantity, revenue trends, and average selling price, then support them with filters and drill-down views for deeper analysis.

## Key Insights

The dashboard highlights that mobile sales analysis is most valuable when it combines summary KPIs with breakdowns by brand, city, payment type, customer age group, and sales channel

The project also shows that monthly trend analysis is important for spotting demand shifts over time and for identifying when sales performance improves or weakens.

![Dashboard Screenshot][(path/to/image.png)](https://github.com/rt5899-art/Mobile_Sales_Analysis/blob/main/Screenshot%202026-06-08%20141916.png?raw=true)

A strong takeaway from the analysis is that decision-making improves when metrics are presented together rather than in isolation, because revenue, units sold, and average selling price tell a more complete story when viewed side by side

## Recommendations for Improvements

- Add more granular KPI tracking for revenue, unit sales, and average selling price by month, brand, and channel.
- Include deeper drill-down filters for city, customer segment, and payment method to support faster comparison.
- Improve layout consistency by grouping related charts together and reducing visual clutter.
- Add trend indicators that show month-over-month growth or decline for major metrics.
- Introduce forecast-based visuals to estimate future sales patterns from historical data.
- Optimize the dashboard for cleaner performance by reducing unnecessary visuals and keeping only the most useful measures.
- Add a dedicated top-products section to make best-selling models easier to identify.
- Expand insights on customer behavior by comparing purchase preferences across age and gender groups.
